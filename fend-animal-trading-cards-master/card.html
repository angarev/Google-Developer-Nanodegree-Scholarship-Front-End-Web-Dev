Labrador

The Labrador Retriever, or just Labrador, is a type of retriever-gun dog. The Lab was first developed in Newfoundland Canada.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus
  • Height: 80 – 85 cm
  • Lifespan: 12–13 years
  • Colour: Black, chocolate, or yellow

The Labrador was used as a working water dog, to help fishermen retrieve nets and haul gear. Their love of water and expert retrieving ability made them ideal waterfowl retrievers for hunters who brought the breed to the U.S. and U.K.
